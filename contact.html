Our Address

Book World Bookstore is conveniently located in the Kitapköy neighborhood of Beylikdüzü, Istanbul. If you're coming from the city center, you can take the E5 Highway and exit at Beylikdüzü. From there, follow the signs to Kitapköy, and you'll find us on Kitap Street. Our bookstore is easily accessible by car or public transportation.

While you're in the Kitapköy neighborhood, be sure to explore the local attractions. You can visit Beylikdüzü Beach for a relaxing day by the sea, or take a stroll in Kitapköy Park, which is just a short walk from our bookstore. If you're a history enthusiast, don't miss the Beylikdüzü History Museum nearby. There are also plenty of cafes and restaurants where you can enjoy a meal or a cup of Turkish tea.
